🌱 About PawVibesZ

PawVibesZ connects people to cats in South Korea, Spain, Saudi Arabia, the United States, Costa Rica, and beyond for short, silent moments of calm. Each 10-minute session is designed as a simple, joyful emotional reset.

@PawsitiveVibesz aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals by promoting mental well-being, empowering women, creating digital jobs, reducing inequalities, and fostering global connection — all through the power of cats on Zoom!
